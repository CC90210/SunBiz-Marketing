# Meta Business Verification Guide — SunBiz Funding

## Why This Is Needed
We've built 5 ad campaigns that are ready to launch on Facebook/Meta. The ads, targeting, budgets ($500 total) — everything is set up. But Meta requires the business to be verified before ads can go live through our automation system. This is a one-time process.

## What You'll Need
- Your **EIN (Employer Identification Number)** or business tax ID
- A **government-issued business document** (ONE of the following):
  - IRS EIN confirmation letter (CP 575 or 147C)
  - State business registration/Articles of Incorporation
  - Business license
  - Business utility bill (with business name and address)
  - Business bank statement (with business name and address)
- Your **business phone number** (Meta will call or text to verify)
- Your **business address** (must match the document you provide)

## Steps (15-20 minutes)

### Step 1: Go to the Business Verification Page
Open this link:
https://business.facebook.com/settings/security/?business_id=1976125016588964

OR go to: https://business.facebook.com/settings/ → Click "Security Center" in the left sidebar → Click "Start Verification" or "Go to verification"

### Step 2: Enter Business Details
- Legal business name: **SunBiz Funding** (must match your documents exactly)
- Business address
- Business phone number
- Business website (if any)
- EIN / Tax ID number

### Step 3: Upload a Business Document
Upload ONE of the documents listed above. Make sure:
- The document shows the business name
- The business name matches what you entered
- The document is not expired
- The file is clear and readable (PDF or photo)

### Step 4: Phone Verification
Meta will call or text the business phone number you provided. Enter the verification code when prompted.

### Step 5: Wait for Review
Meta typically reviews and approves within 1-3 business days. You'll get a notification when it's approved.

## After Verification Is Approved
Let your developer know. They will:
1. Publish the app to "Live" mode (takes 1 click after verification)
2. Launch all 5 ad campaigns automatically via API
3. Ads will start running immediately

## Important Notes
- This is a ONE-TIME process — you never have to do it again
- This does NOT give anyone else access to your bank account or financial info
- This is standard for all businesses running ads through Meta's API
- If you have questions during the process, contact Meta Business Help: https://www.facebook.com/business/help
