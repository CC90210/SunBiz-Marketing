Data Deletion Instructions

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data that SunBiz Funding holds, including data collected through our website, inquiry forms, and advertising platforms such as Meta (Facebook and Instagram). This page explains what data may be held, how to submit a deletion request, and what to expect after you do.

01 — What Data We May Hold

Depending on how you have interacted with SunBiz Funding, we may hold some or all of the following categories of personal data:

  • Name, email address, and phone number submitted through inquiry forms
  • Business details provided during the funding inquiry process (business name, industry, revenue, funding needs)
  • Email engagement data (opens, clicks) from marketing communications
  • Website interaction data collected via cookies and analytics tools
  • Ad interaction data collected via the Meta Pixel or Meta Conversions API
  • Any correspondence you have sent to us directly

02 — Meta / Facebook Data

Meta Platform

If you have interacted with our advertisements on Facebook or Instagram, Meta (Meta Platforms, Inc.) may have collected data about those interactions as part of its own advertising infrastructure. This data is held by Meta and governed by Meta's Data Policy.

SunBiz Funding uses the Meta Pixel and Meta Conversions API for ad measurement only. We do not receive or store raw user-level behavioral data from Meta directly.

To request deletion of data held by Meta directly, you should:

  • Visit your Facebook account settings at facebook.com/settings
  • Navigate to "Your Facebook Information" and select "Off-Facebook Activity"
  • Use the "Clear History" and "Manage Future Activity" tools to limit or remove data
  • Submit a data deletion request directly to Meta via Meta's support portal

To request that SunBiz Funding remove any data we hold that originated from Meta integrations, follow the process in Section 03 below.

03 — How to Submit a Deletion Request

To request deletion of your personal data held by SunBiz Funding, follow the steps below. We do not require you to create an account or log in to submit a request.

  1. 1
    Compose an email Send an email to info@sunbizfunding.com from the email address associated with your inquiry or interaction with us.
  2. 2
    Use the required subject line Set your subject line to exactly: Data Deletion Request — this ensures your message reaches the correct team and is logged appropriately.
  3. 3
    Include identifying information In the body of your email, include your full name, the email address(es) used when contacting us or submitting an inquiry, and optionally your phone number or business name so we can locate all records associated with you.
  4. 4
    Specify the scope of your request Indicate whether you want all data deleted, or only specific categories (e.g., marketing lists only, or inquiry form data only).
  5. 5
    Receive confirmation We will acknowledge your request within 5 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days. We will send a confirmation email when deletion is complete.

04 — What Happens After Your Request

Once we receive your verified deletion request, we will:

  • Locate all personal data associated with your identifying information across our systems
  • Permanently delete or anonymize that data within 30 days
  • Instruct any sub-processors who hold your data on our behalf to delete it as well
  • Remove your email address from all marketing lists immediately upon receipt of your request
  • Send you a written confirmation once deletion is complete

Please note that we may be required by law to retain certain records for a defined period (for example, financial records or records related to a prior contractual relationship). In those cases, we will inform you of what records we are legally obligated to retain and for how long.

We will not charge any fee for processing a data deletion request. We will not discriminate against you in any way for exercising this right.

05 — Identity Verification

To protect against unauthorized deletion requests, we may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request. Verification is typically done by confirming the email address associated with your records. We will never ask for sensitive financial information, passwords, or government ID unless legally required and will explain the reason if we do.

If you are submitting a request on behalf of another person (for example, as a legal representative), please state this in your email and include documentation of your authority to act on their behalf.
