# SKILL: Ad Copywriting for MCA / Business Funding

> High-converting ad copy for SunBiz Funding's MCA consolidation and growth capital campaigns.

---

## Core Principles
1. **Lead with pain point, not product** — "Stop the daily drain" not "We offer MCA consolidation"
2. **Educate, don't sell** — Position as financial advisor sharing knowledge
3. **Daily affordability over total cost** — "Reduce your daily payment" not "Lower your factor rate"
4. **Build trust through transparency** — "We analyze your bank statements and show you what we see"
5. **Compliance always** — Never say "loan," never promise guaranteed approval

## Language Rules (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
| ALWAYS Use | NEVER Use | Why |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| Advance | Loan | MCA is legally not a loan |
| Funding / Capital | Lending / Money | Professional positioning |
| Consolidation | Refinance | MCA industry terminology |
| Merchant | Borrower | Industry standard |
| Daily payment | Interest rate | MCA uses factor rates, not APR |
| "See if you qualify" | "Guaranteed approval" | Compliance |

## Copy Frameworks

### PAS (Problem → Agitate → Solution)
```
Problem: "Carrying 3+ MCA positions?"
Agitate: "That's $1,500-$3,000 leaving your account every day — before payroll, rent, or inventory."
Solution: "SunBiz Funding consolidates your positions into one manageable daily payment."
```

### Multi-Phase Education (SunBiz Signature)
```
Hook: "Overleveraged? We don't stack — we fix."
Phase 1: Immediate relief — consolidate daily payments
Phase 2: Buy out multiple positions
Phase 3: Become the sole funder — one payment
Phase 4: Transition to a true Line of Credit
CTA: "See where you are on the path →"
```

### Before/After (Consolidation)
```
Before: "4 MCA positions. $2,100/day in ACH debits. Cash flow choking."
After: "1 position. $850/day. $1,250/day back in your business."
CTA: "Get your consolidation plan."
```

### Objection Pre-empt
```
Rate: "It's not about the rate. It's about what you can afford daily."
Trust: "We analyze your bank statements and show you exactly what we see."
Stack: "We don't add to the pile. We buy out your positions."
```

## COMPLIANCE RED LINES (NEVER Write)
- "Guaranteed approval" / "Everyone qualifies" — NEVER
- "Loan" / "Lending" / "Lender" — NEVER (for MCA products)
- "Interest rate" / "APR" — NEVER (MCA uses factor rates)
- Specific factor rates without client approval — NEVER
- "Refinance" — NEVER (use "consolidate")
- Fake urgency: "Only 3 spots left" — NEVER

## SAFE MCA Copy Patterns
- "See if you qualify"
- "Get your free analysis"
- "Reduce your daily payments"
- "We analyze your bank statements"
- "Consolidate your positions"
- "One payment. One partner."
