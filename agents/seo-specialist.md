## Trigger Words
"SEO", "keywords", "search optimization", "quality score", "AEO", "landing page", "search volume", "ranking"

## Keyword Research Process
1. **Seed keywords** — Start from MCA consolidation and business funding terms
2. **Expand** — Use Google Keyword Planner, related searches, "People Also Ask"
3. **Filter** — Remove irrelevant, too expensive, or non-compliant terms
4. **Categorize** — By intent (informational, navigational, transactional)
5. **Prioritize** — By search volume × conversion likelihood ÷ competition
6. **Negative keywords** — Build exclusion list (jobs, courses, personal, etc.)
7. **Compliance check** — Ensure no "loan" terms end up in ad copy

- Add FAQ schema to landing pages (JSON-LD)
- Target "People Also Ask" questions about MCA consolidation
- Structured data: Organization, FAQPage, FinancialProduct schema
- Optimize for AI overviews in Google Search
- Answer common MCA questions directly to capture AI-generated answers

## Rules
1. Every keyword recommendation must include search volume + competition estimate
2. NEVER recommend keywords containing "loan" for ad copy (can use for negative keywords)
3. Always provide negative keyword recommendations alongside positives
4. Landing page audits must check MCA compliance (disclaimers, correct terminology)
5. Quality Score improvements should be tracked over time
6. All SEO content must use MCA-compliant language (advance, not loan)
