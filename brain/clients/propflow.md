# PropFlow — Client Profile

## Identity
- **Product:** Real estate / property management SaaS
- **Ownership:** 50/50 CC + Adon (partner, handles networking & connections)
- **Stage:** Pre-revenue / early beta (2026-Q2)
- **Tech:** Next.js 14, Supabase, Stripe

## Target ICP
- **Primary:** Small property management firms (5–50 doors) in Ontario initially, Canada-wide later
- **Secondary:** Independent landlords with 2+ rentals who want screening + rent collection without hiring a PM
- **Decision maker:** PM owner, 35–60 y/o, currently patching problems with spreadsheets + SingleKey + manual emails

## Competitive Landscape
- **SingleKey** (147K+ Canadian landlords, SOC 2). Strengths: screening forms, tenant reports, rent guarantee. Weaknesses: no CRM/PM backbone, no workflow automation, Ontario-centric.
- **Buildium / AppFolio**: US-focused, expensive, enterprise.
- **PropFlow's edge:** screening data integration (Certn API) + tenant-facing portal + workflow automation. Skip rent guarantee initially (requires insurance partner).

## Brand Voice
- **Register:** Professional but not stuffy. Real estate owners hate being talked down to.
- **Tone:** Operator-to-operator. "We know your business because we've been through the spreadsheets too."
- **No "revolutionary" or "game-changing"** — PM folks are skeptical of hype.
