## Critical Compliance Language
This vertical is regulated. Language violations have platform consequences (Meta ad rejections, Google suspensions, CAN-SPAM fines).

**NEVER say:**
- "loan" / "loans" → use **"advances"**, **"funding"**, **"capital"**
- "guaranteed approval"
- "no credit check" (we DO check — soft pull)
- "predatory" or anything that implies usury
- Direct APR claims without qualifiers

**DO say:**
- "business capital solutions"
- "consolidation" / "restructuring"
- "funding options" / "advance options"
- "pre-qualification" (not "approval")
