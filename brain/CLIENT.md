## Company Overview

- **Role Positioning:** Financial Advisor & Strategic Capital Partner (NOT a transactional broker)

## Products & Services

### Primary: MCA Consolidation / Restructuring
The core offering that differentiates SunBiz from competitors:
- **Multi-Phase Consolidation Strategy:**
  - *Phase 1:* Immediate relief/consolidation — reduce daily payment burden
  - *Phase 2:* Buy out multiple existing MCA positions
  - *Phase 3:* Become the sole funder — single payment, single relationship
  - *Phase 4:* Transition to a true Line of Credit (LOC) — best terms, lowest cost
- **Target:** Overleveraged merchants carrying 2-5 existing MCA positions with 30%-50% leverage ratios

### Secondary: Growth Capital / A-Paper Funding
- Clean, fast capital for businesses with minimal existing positions
- Revenue-based financing for expansion, equipment, inventory
- Quick decisions, fast funding without bank delays or equity dilution

## Key Differentiators

### Language Rules (CRITICAL — External Communications)
| Use | Do NOT Use | Why |
|-----|-----------|-----|
| Private lending | Loan | Not a traditional loan product |
| Working capital | Merchant Cash Advance / MCA | Never mention MCA in external comms |
| Business funding / Business capital | Money | Professional positioning |
| Debt consolidation | Refinance | We consolidate, not refinance |
| No credit pull / No UCC | No hard credit pull | Simpler, cleaner messaging |
| Funding | Lending (in product context) | Professional positioning |
| Capital partner | Lender | Relationship framing |
| Business owner | Borrower / Merchant | Customer-facing language |

**KEY RULE:** In all customer-facing materials (emails, ads, landing pages), NEVER use "Merchant Cash Advance", "MCA", or industry jargon. Use "private lending", "working capital", "business funding" instead. Internal docs may reference MCA for operational clarity.

### Key Messages (Ranked by Use)
1. "Overleveraged? We build a multi-phase path to financial health."
2. "Stop the daily drain — consolidate your MCA positions."
3. "From multiple funders to one. From daily debits to breathing room."
4. "We don't stack another advance on top of your debt. We fix it."
5. "Smart capital for growing businesses."
6. "We analyze your bank statements to find the safest path to capital."
7. "It's not about the rate — it's about what you can afford daily."

### Objection Handling in Ad Copy
- **"Rate" Objection:** Never focus on factor rates (cents on the dollar). Pivot to **daily affordability** and **cash flow impact**
  - *Use:* "It's not about the rate; it's about what you can afford to pay daily without hurting your operations."
- **"Another MCA?" Objection:** We're not stacking — we're consolidating
  - *Use:* "We don't add to the pile. We buy out your positions and reduce your daily obligation."
- **"Can I trust you?" Objection:** Data-driven transparency
  - *Use:* "We analyze your bank statements and show you exactly what we see. No surprises."

- **NEVER use "loan"** when referring to MCA products — use "advance," "funding," "capital"
- **NEVER promise guaranteed approvals** — use "See if you qualify"
- **NEVER guarantee specific terms** without disclaimers
- Must imply **data-driven underwriting**: "We analyze your bank statements to find the safest path to capital"

### Meta Ads Special Ad Category
- **MUST use `special_ad_categories: ['CREDIT']`** for all funding ads
